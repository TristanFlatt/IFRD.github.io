About International Female Ride Day

About Us

International Female Ride Day (IFRD) is a global campaign for women motorcyclists who own, ride, and enjoy motorcycles. The event occurs annually on the first Saturday of May. IFRD aims to highlight the diverse and growing number of women riders while encouraging other women to take up the sport.

Since its inception, IFRD has grown to include participants from all over the world, celebrating the freedom and joy of riding motorcycles. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a beginner, IFRD is a day to come together and share your passion for motorcycling.
